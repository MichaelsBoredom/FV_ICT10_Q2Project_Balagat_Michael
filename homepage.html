About Our School

Metropolitan University of Caloocan is a high-ranking school that primarily focuses on the education of the young and the production of the future leaven. With this field, we are more than thirty years old and still, we are very proud of our tradition of academic excellence as well as holistic development.

It is the partnership of our top-notch facilities, very capable teachers, and innovative curriculum that helps students to shine not only in academics but also in the areas of social and emotional growth.
